Welcome to Frogger

The object of the game is to reach the water, without touching any bugs! You can move your character by pressing the up, down, left and right arrow keys.

You get one point for reaching the water and lose one point for touching a bug! There are bonus points for collecting the treasures as you go. You win the game when you have collected 5 points.

Please select your character to get started!
